# Incident #01 — Network Outage

## Date: March 2025
## Severity: High
## Status: Resolved

---

## Problem
Users in the office reported they could not access the internet or internal servers.
All workstations lost network connectivity at 9:15 AM.

---

## Initial Symptoms
- Ping to 8.8.8.8 (Google DNS) failed
- Internal file server unreachable
- No IP address assigned to workstations (APIPA 169.254.x.x detected)
